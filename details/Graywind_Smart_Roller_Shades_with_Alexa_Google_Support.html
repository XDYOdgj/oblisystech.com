Graywind Smart Roller Shades: Style, Privacy, and Smart Home Integration

Transform your space with shades that deliver total blackout privacy, energy efficiency, and seamless smart control.

Why Choose Graywind Smart Roller Shades?

Featuring a four-weave blackout fabric, Graywind Smart Roller Shades offer complete privacy by blocking out all natural light. The added thermal insulation layer helps regulate indoor temperatures, making your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter. This translates to reduced energy bills and greater comfort year-round.

Designed with safety in mind, the cordless mechanism eliminates the risk of entanglement, making it an ideal choice for households with children or pets. Customizable window sizes ensure a perfect fit, while the included valance and bottom bar let you enhance the look of any room with a personalized touch.

Smart Control at Your Fingertips

Graywind Smart Roller Shades are fully compatible with Alexa and Google Assistant, allowing you to control them using simple voice commands. This integration makes it easy to manage your lighting preferences without leaving your seat.

The 15-channel remote control gives you the ability to program preferred positions, set group controls, and manage multiple shades effortlessly. A clear battery indicator on the remote lets you know when it's time to recharge. For those who love automation, the 2.4GHz Zigbee hub enables scheduling and smart home integration, giving you complete control over your environment.

Upgrade your interior with shades that are as smart as they are stylish. Graywind offers the perfect blend of functionality and design for your bedroom, living room, or media room.

Upgrade Your Home with Smart Blinds That Deliver Style and Safety

Introducing the WEFFORT 5% openness smart blinds, designed to enhance your living space with cutting-edge technology and elegant design. These blinds feature a high-performance sun shade fabric that offers UV protection up to 95%, effectively blocking harmful rays while maintaining a clear view of the outdoors. Ideal for homes that value both comfort and modern functionality, they are engineered to provide optimal light control and energy efficiency.

Equipped with a premium battery-powered motor, these blinds allow effortless control through voice commands on Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit. The smart hub technology ensures seamless integration with your existing smart home setup, letting you adjust the blinds using your phone or by simply speaking. Whether you're managing your home schedule or relaxing in your favorite room, these blinds make it simple and intuitive.

With a quiet operation of less than 35db, these blinds are perfect for peaceful environments like bedrooms, living rooms, or home offices. Their advanced Thread and Matter compatible motor ensures reliable, safe, and stable performance, while the rechargeable battery guarantees up to 4-6 months of use on a single 6-hour charge. The USB charging option adds versatility and convenience, supporting various methods such as solar panels, USB ports, or standard power adapters.

6-Piece Universal Curtain Rod Brackets

These versatile and durable curtain rod brackets are perfect for a wide range of installations. Constructed from high-quality PC plastic and stainless steel, they provide reliable support for your curtains or rods. The strong 3M adhesive makes them ideal for mounting on smooth surfaces like glass, tile, and marble, while the sleek black finish adds a modern and stylish touch to any room.

Features

  • Universal fit for curtain rods 0.6'' to 1.5'' in diameter
  • Durable PC plastic and stainless steel construction
  • Powerful 3M adhesive for strong wall mounting
  • Easy installation with optional screw reinforcement
  • Suitable for bathroom, kitchen, and living room use

Why Choose Us

Designed for maximum strength and versatility, these brackets are a must-have for anyone looking to upgrade their home decor. Whether you're securing a towel rod in your kitchen, installing curtains in your bathroom, or holding a window treatment in your living room, these brackets provide safe and secure support with minimal installation effort. They can hold up to 14 pounds when using adhesive or 20 pounds with screw reinforcement, ensuring long-term reliability and durability.

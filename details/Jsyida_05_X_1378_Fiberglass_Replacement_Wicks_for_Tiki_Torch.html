Jsyida Replacement Wicks for Tiki Torch & Outdoor Lighting

Perfect for creating a warm and inviting atmosphere in your outdoor space

Reinforced with durable brass mounts, they offer a secure fit and are easy to install, turning any glass container into a stylish lighting fixture.

Upgrade your outdoor decor with these DIY-friendly wicks for wine bottle or glass container use. All you need is to attach the wick holders, add a rubber ring for stability, and fill with your preferred oil.
